Our Business Models

We offer comprehensive solutions for the global mobile market through three core business pillars.

Project Operation

We provide full-lifecycle operation services for external projects. From user acquisition to community management and data analysis, we help your product reach its maximum potential in the global market.

Project Development

Our R&D center specializes in high-performance mobile application and game development. We take your ideas and turn them into market-ready products with cutting-edge technology.

Self-developed Operations

We develop and operate our own portfolio of mobile products. Our focus is on high-quality user experiences combined with sophisticated monetization models.

Monetization Expertise

Our products leverage diverse and effective monetization strategies designed for global audiences:

  • In-App Subscriptions: Recurring revenue models for premium content.
  • Virtual Items: In-game and in-app purchases for enhanced user experience.
  • Advanced Features: Pay-per-feature models for power users.
  • VIP Systems: Loyalty-based membership tiers.
  • Ad Monetization: Integrated advertising solutions (rewarded video, interstitial, etc.).
